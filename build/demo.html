Kipling C. Dunlap

Born and raised in the Atlanta, GA area; Kip holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Public Policy Analysis from Georgia State University, and received a certificate in Full-Stack Web Development coding from Georgia Tech's Bootcamp in December of 2021.

Kip spent the better part of a decade working in the Land Use and Public Transit Advocacy fields as a practitioner of Transportation Demand Management ("TDM") and was a member of Americans for Commuter Transportation and served on the board of the Atlanta planning advocacy group, ThreadATL and serves as Chief Legislative Aide to Representative Park Cannon.
